Frequently Asked Questions

Purchasing on Homemade

How to Purchase an Item On Homemade ?

To purchase an item on Homemade:

  • Find an item you want to purchase on the Homemade website or mobile app
  • Choose the options you want for that item, if applicable.
  • Click Add to cart.
  • Choose your preferred payment method under How you’ll pay.
  • Click Proceed to checkout.
  • Confirm your shipping address and payment details.
  • Review your order to check that your information and order are correct.
  • Click Place your order.

When you buy on Homemade, you're purchasing from individual Homemade partners. If you have any questions about an item or order, reach out to Homemade Support.

To authenticate a payment, you'll need to respond to a prompt from your card issuer and provide additional information. Depending on your card issuer, this additional information may include a correct response to a question, a password, or a passcode.

Congratulations!

Homemade will send you an email for order that you’ve placed. If you have any questions about your order, be sure to reach out to Homemade Support.

I'm Having a Technical Problem with Buying on Homemade

If you’re experiencing an issue with your order, you must work with Homemade Support to try to resolve the problem. You can contact us through the in-app chat or send us an email at support@fromhomemade.com or call us

How to Get Help Before You Order

What Is the Processing Time for an Item?

The processing time is the length of time a Homemade partner needs to get an order ready to ship, or the length of time between when you place an order and when the partner ships your order. These might be more than usual because at Homemade, each order is custom-made by hand for you.

Homemade Partners can set the processing times for items in their offerings. The processing time can vary from item to item for each Partner.

You can find the processing time for an item on the item’s listing page:

  • Click an item you’re interested in on Homemade mobile app.
  • On the listing page, you’ll see either:
    • Ready to ship in: This shows the processing time for the item.
    • Estimated delivery date: The date range when you can expect this order to arrive.

Shipping

How to Track Your Homemade Order ?

You can check the shipping status of your order on the Homemade app. If there is tracking information available, you can check when your order should arrive.

To track your purchase:

  • Sign in to the Homemade app.
  • Click the Your Account icon.
  • Click Purchases and reviews.
  • Find your order.
  • You’ll see the shipping status to the right of your order:
    • Not Shipped: The Homemade Partner hasn’t shipped the order yet or didn’t update the order on Homemade.
    • Shipped: The Homemade Partner marked the order as shipped.
    • In Transit: The Homemade Partner added a tracking number and the package has left the shipping facility.
    • Track package: If the order has a tracking number, you’ll see Track Package. Click Track Package to see more tracking updates.

You can also see shipping information in the shipping notification emails for your order. Click Track Package in the email notification to view the status of your delivery.

Why wasn't tracking information provided for my order?

All orders placed on Homemade include tracking information. But there might be times when due to technical issues, tracking information might not be available. You can contact Homemade Support for information of your order.

Cart & Payment

I'm Having Trouble Checking Out

If you’re having trouble checking out on Homemade, check these common issues:

  • I’m seeing an error when I try to check out
  • My order is still processing
I’m seeing an error when I try to check out

If you see the error: "There was a problem with your order. Please try again, if you continue to have problems, please contact support.

Then: Review the billing and shipping information you entered and check that it’s correct. If it is correct, click Contact support at the bottom of this page.

My order is still processing

If you received an email from Homemade saying that your Homemade order is still processing, it means that your purchase is being screened by Homemade’s third party partner. All Homemade Payments orders are screened to ensure that the orders are legitimate and to protect from possible fraud.

Most orders are processed in under 72 hours. You’ll receive a confirmation email when the review is complete.

Why is there a charge on my card if my order is still processing?

You may see a charge on your credit card or bank statement while we work to process your order. The charge will only be finalized when the purchase has been processed successfully.

What Payment Methods Can I Use to Check Out on Homemade?

Homemade supports Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, Paytm and other wallets, UPI via BHIM UPI , Google Pay, PhonePe etc.

You may see a pending transaction on your credit card bill for a small amount. This is a pre-authorization test to ensure your card is valid. Once your card has been validated, it will be dropped from your statement.
